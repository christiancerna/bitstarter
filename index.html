This Project is an App than will allow to you keep you own tracking of your swimming times all the four styles as well as view swimming time standards. Share swimming race times with friends and family using your favorite social networking toolsFacebook.

We need your support to make this project real.

FEATURES

  • Single swimming time standards
  • Race Time Charts for each swim type
  • Swim race types include Freestyle, Breaststroke, Backstroke, Butterfly
  • You can select between Long Course Meters (LCM), Short Course Meters (SCM), Short Course Yards (SCY) type of pools.
  • Record and log your own swim times
  • You can compared your performance with friends just with invited then to use TiemposDeNatacion
